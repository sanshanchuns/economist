The time of year has come again when gift-givers <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">trudge</span> around shops and <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">scroll</span> <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">endlessly</span> through <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">e-commerce</span> websites to find <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">suitable</span> Christmas presents for their loved ones. Some will enjoy <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">browsing</span>. But many will not. A growing number will happily <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">outsource</span> much of the process to artificial intelligence (AI).<br/>

<span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Chatbots</span> offer a personal shopper for all. They can listen to what a user wants, produce a <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">shortlist</span> of products and help with <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">comparisons</span>. This holiday season around two-thirds of <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">consumers</span> in rich countries, and five-sixths of those aged 18-24, plan to use AI to help them shop, according to a survey by Shopify, a provider of <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">e-commerce</span> tools. A study from McKinsey, a <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">consultancy</span>, found that the second-most-common use for ChatGPT-like "<span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">generative</span>" AI in America is for shopping advice, behind general research but ahead of writing assistance (see chart 1). Increasingly, <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">consumers</span> can even make <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">purchases</span> directly through a chatbot. By 2030 McKinsey <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">reckons</span> that<br/>

AI firms are <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">betting</span> that their technology will <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">disrupt</span> shopping just as e- commerce did in the internet age. Although OpenAI has <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">deprioritised</span> efforts to <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">integrate</span> ads into ChatGPT, it has struck deals with Shopify and Etsy, an online <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">marketplace</span> for <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">artisanal</span> <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">wares</span>, to allow <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">merchants</span> to sell their products through its chatbot <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">in return for</span> a fee. Google, whose search engine has long been the starting point for many shoppers, is also eyeing the market for so-called <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">agentic</span> commerce. Users in America can have its AI tools call shops to check <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">stock</span>, track the prices of products and make some <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">purchases</span> <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">on their behalf</span> when deals <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">come up</span>.<br/>

Others have been more <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">open-minded</span>.
