<span style="color:#E3120B; font-size:14.9pt; font-weight:bold;">By Invitation</span> <span style="color:#000000; font-size:14.9pt; font-weight:bold;">| Winds of change</span>
<span style="color:#000000; font-size:21.0pt; font-weight:bold;">The climate action that matters is in the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">global south</span>, argues an <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">architect</span> of the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Paris agreement</span></span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:14.9pt; font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">But, writes <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Christiana Figueres</span>, <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">innovation</span> still has to <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">outpace</span> <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">climate impacts</span></span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:6.2pt;">November 6th 2025</span>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **global south**：全球南方，指发展中国家和新兴经济体，与"全球北方"（发达国家）相对。这一术语强调地理、经济和政治上的南北差异。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **architect**：建筑师、设计者。此处指巴黎协定的主要设计者和推动者，强调作者在协定形成过程中的关键作用。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **Paris agreement**：巴黎协定，2015年通过的历史性气候协议，195个国家承诺将全球升温控制在2摄氏度以内，并努力限制在1.5摄氏度。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **Christiana Figueres**：克里斯蒂安娜·菲格雷斯，哥斯达黎加外交官，2010-2016年担任《联合国气候变化框架公约》执行秘书，是巴黎协定的主要推动者之一。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **innovation**：创新。此处特指清洁能源技术的创新，与气候冲击形成对比。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **outpace**：超过、跑赢。强调创新的速度必须快于气候变化的负面影响。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **climate impacts**：气候影响、气候冲击。指气候变化带来的各种负面影响，如极端天气、海平面上升等。</span>
</div>

![](../images/011_The_climate_action_that_matters_is_in_the_global_south_argue/p0046_img01.jpeg)

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;"><span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">AS WE APPROACH COP30</span> in <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Belém</span>, <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Brazil</span>, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">we stand</span> at a <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">hinge</span> in history. On the one hand, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">emissions are still rising</span>, with devastating consequences, and <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">national climate plans are woefully behind</span> where they need to be. But <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">these plans reflect</span> political <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">calculation</span> rather than economic reality—because, on the other hand, the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">exponential curves</span> of technology, economics and human <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">ingenuity</span> <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">are bending</span> towards a future where affordable, clean energy for all can finally be a reality. Even as the politics have stalled, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">it is the economy and the hope of people everywhere that keep pulling change forward</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **COP30**：第30届联合国气候变化大会（Conference of the Parties），将于2025年在巴西贝伦举行。COP是《联合国气候变化框架公约》的缔约方会议。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **Belém**：贝伦，巴西北部帕拉州首府，位于亚马孙河入海口，是2025年COP30的举办地。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **hinge**：铰链、转折点。此处比喻历史的关键转折时刻，强调COP30的重要性。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **calculation**：计算、算计。此处指政治上的权衡和考量，与"经济现实"形成对比，暗示政治决策可能滞后于经济实际。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **exponential curves**：指数曲线。数学概念，指增长速度呈指数级增长的趋势，强调技术、经济和创新的加速发展。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **ingenuity**：创造力、聪明才智。指人类在解决气候问题上的创新能力。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;">Ten years ago in France, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">195 countries stood together and chose</span> a clear direction. Against long <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">odds</span>, in the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Paris agreement</span>—the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">negotiations</span> which I led as <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">executive secretary</span> of the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">UN Framework Convention on Climate Change</span>—<span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">they committed themselves</span> to limit <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">global heating</span>, protect the most <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">vulnerable</span> and <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">unleash</span> an unprecedented economic <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">transformation</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">That transformation is now unmistakably under way</span>, despite a global <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">pandemic</span>, war, <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Brexit</span> and two <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Trump presidencies</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **odds**：概率、可能性。Against long odds指在不利条件下，强调达成协定的困难。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **negotiations**：谈判。作者作为执行秘书主导了巴黎协定的谈判过程，这是她个人经历的直接体现。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **executive secretary**：执行秘书。联合国框架公约下的高级职位，负责协调和推动气候谈判。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **UN Framework Convention on Climate Change**：联合国气候变化框架公约，简称UNFCCC，1992年通过，是国际气候治理的基础框架。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **global heating**：全球升温。比"global warming"更直接，强调问题的紧迫性。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **vulnerable**：脆弱的、易受伤害的。指最容易受到气候变化影响的人群和地区，如小岛国、贫困地区等。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **unleash**：释放、发动。强调经济转型的巨大潜力和影响力。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **transformation**：转型、转变。指从化石燃料经济向清洁能源经济的根本性转变。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **pandemic**：大流行。指2020-2022年的COVID-19疫情，对全球经济和社会造成巨大冲击。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **Brexit**：英国脱欧。2016年公投决定，2020年正式完成，对欧洲和全球政治经济格局产生深远影响。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **Trump presidencies**：特朗普政府。指2017-2021年和2025年至今的两届特朗普政府，对气候政策持怀疑态度。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;">In 2015 <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">global CO₂ emissions were still rising</span> by almost 2% per year; <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">that growth has since slowed</span> to 0.3%. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">Fossil-fuel demand has plateaued</span> and <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">is falling</span> in several large economies, including China. Then, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">the world was on course</span> for about 4°C of <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">warming</span> by 2100. Today, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">projections hover</span> near 2.6°C—still dangerously high, but a profound <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">course correction</span> that must now deepen, and fast.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **CO₂**：二氧化碳，化学式CO₂，是主要的温室气体之一，导致全球变暖的主要原因。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **warming**：升温。指全球平均气温的上升，4°C的升温将带来灾难性后果。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **course correction**：路线修正。航海术语，指调整航向，此处比喻气候政策的重大调整。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;">Even as the warnings from scientists grow more urgent, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">the economic transformation is accelerating</span>—and <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">shifting global power dynamics</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">The world's clean-energy future is no longer being decided</span> in the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">boardrooms</span> of the old <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">energy powers</span>, but in the bustling cities and <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">industrial corridors</span> of the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">global south</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">A new economy—a new kind of growth—is rising</span>, powered by clean electricity, <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">digital innovation</span> and new <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">energy technologies</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **boardrooms**：董事会会议室。象征传统能源公司的决策中心，强调权力从传统能源巨头向新兴经济体的转移。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **energy powers**：能源强国。指传统上依赖化石燃料并控制全球能源市场的国家，如美国、俄罗斯、沙特等。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **industrial corridors**：工业走廊。指连接多个工业城市的带状区域，是新兴制造业和清洁能源产业的重要载体。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **digital innovation**：数字创新。指人工智能、物联网、大数据等技术在能源领域的应用。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **energy technologies**：能源技术。包括太阳能、风能、储能、氢能等清洁能源技术。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;"><span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">The Trump administration clearly sees this as a threat</span> to the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">fossil-fuel sector</span> and its own <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">dominance</span>, hence <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">its attempts to quash the rise of renewables</span> at home and <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">aggressively intervene</span> in <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">multilateral climate talks</span>. But <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">the transformation is unstoppable</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">A new industrial sunbelt is fast being born</span>, from Latin America to Australia through Africa and Asia. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">This sunbelt now hosts</span> nearly half of <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">investment-ready</span> <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">clean-industry projects</span> outside China, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">sparking new industries</span>—for <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">green ammonia</span>, <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">fertiliser</span> and <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">clean fuels</span>—<span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">creating skilled jobs</span>, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">strengthening energy security</span> and <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">opening up new export markets</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **fossil-fuel sector**：化石燃料行业。包括石油、天然气、煤炭等传统能源产业。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **dominance**：主导地位、霸权。指美国在传统能源领域的领导地位和影响力。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **multilateral climate talks**：多边气候谈判。指联合国框架下的国际气候会议，如COP会议。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **sunbelt**：阳光带。原指美国南部阳光充足的地区，此处比喻新兴的清洁能源产业带，横跨多个大洲。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **investment-ready**：准备就绪的投资项目。指已经完成可行性研究、获得许可、可以立即开始建设的项目。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **clean-industry projects**：清洁产业项目。指使用清洁能源的制造业项目，如绿色钢铁、绿色化工等。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **green ammonia**：绿色氨。使用可再生能源生产的氨，可用作肥料和燃料，是未来重要的清洁能源载体。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **fertiliser**：肥料。绿色氨可用作肥料，替代传统化石燃料生产的氨肥。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **clean fuels**：清洁燃料。包括绿色氢、生物燃料、合成燃料等，用于替代化石燃料。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;"><span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">The global south holds</span> 70% of the world's wind and <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">solar potential</span>, and 50% of the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">minerals</span> necessary for the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">energy transition</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">China sees the opportunity</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">Its solar exports to the global south have doubled</span> in the past two years, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">surpassing sales to the global north</span> for the first time since 2018. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">Shipments to Africa alone surged</span> by 60% last year. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">These panels don't just generate electricity</span>; <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">they generate huge savings</span> for people and businesses. In Nigeria, for example, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">savings from avoiding expensive diesel can repay the cost of a solar panel</span> within six months.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **solar potential**：太阳能潜力。指一个地区接收太阳辐射的能力，决定太阳能发电的可行性。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **minerals**：矿产。能源转型所需的关键矿产包括锂、钴、镍（用于电池）、稀土元素（用于风力发电机和电动汽车）等。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **energy transition**：能源转型。指从化石燃料向可再生能源的根本性转变。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;">Pakistan, only recently almost entirely dependent on <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">fossil fuels</span>, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">now expects solar to supply</span> 20% of its electricity by next year. Iran <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">has just committed</span> <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">$2.3bn</span> to expand <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">solar power</span> as a "<span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">strategic necessity</span>". In <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Oman</span> <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">the share of renewables in electricity production more than doubled</span> in a <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">frenetic</span> first five months of 2025, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">helping the country's efforts to turn itself into</span> a global <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">green-steel hub</span>, powered by locally produced <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">green hydrogen</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **fossil fuels**：化石燃料。包括石油、天然气、煤炭等，是导致气候变化的主要碳排放源。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **$2.3bn**：23亿美元。bn是billion的缩写，表示十亿。美元符号在HTML中需转义为&#36;，但此处为注释中的说明，使用普通文本即可。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **solar power**：太阳能发电。利用光伏技术将太阳光转化为电能。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **strategic necessity**：战略必要性。强调太阳能对伊朗国家安全的战略重要性，而非仅仅是经济考量。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **Oman**：阿曼，中东国家，传统上依赖石油出口，现正积极发展可再生能源。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **frenetic**：狂热的、疯狂的。形容2025年前五个月可再生能源发展的快速节奏。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **green-steel hub**：绿色钢铁中心。使用可再生能源（如绿色氢）生产的钢铁，碳排放远低于传统钢铁。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **green hydrogen**：绿色氢。使用可再生能源（如太阳能、风能）通过电解水生产的氢气，是未来重要的清洁能源载体。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;">Meanwhile, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">advances in battery storage for solar energy are rapidly displacing gas expansion</span>, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">leaving the "bridge fuel" theory looking increasingly outdated</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">This is the "self-propelling global uptake with exponential growth of installed capacity"</span> that <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Tim Lenton</span>, a <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">climate scientist</span>, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">has identified</span> as a positive <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">tipping point</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">Each step forward in deployment accelerates the next</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **battery storage**：电池储能。指用于储存太阳能和风能产生的电力的电池系统，解决了可再生能源间歇性的问题。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **bridge fuel**：桥梁燃料。指天然气作为从化石燃料向可再生能源过渡的"桥梁"，这一理论认为天然气比煤炭更清洁，可以作为过渡能源。但随着储能技术进步，这一理论受到质疑。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **Tim Lenton**：蒂姆·伦顿，英国埃克塞特大学气候科学家，以研究气候临界点（tipping points）而闻名。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **climate scientist**：气候科学家。专门研究气候系统、气候变化及其影响的科学家。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **tipping point**：临界点、转折点。指系统从一个稳定状态快速转向另一个稳定状态的关键时刻。在气候科学中，指一旦超过某个阈值，气候变化将变得不可逆转。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;"><span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">Renewables are growing</span> at record speed: <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">the world installed</span> 15 times more <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">solar capacity</span> in 2024 than the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">IEA</span> <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">had predicted it would</span> in a <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">forecast</span> in 2015; <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">wind has exceeded the forecast</span> three-fold. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">Clean energy now employs more people than fossil fuels</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **solar capacity**：太阳能装机容量。指已安装的太阳能发电系统的总发电能力，通常以吉瓦（GW）或太瓦（TW）为单位。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **IEA**：国际能源署（International Energy Agency），总部位于巴黎，是经合组织框架下的能源政策咨询机构，定期发布能源预测报告。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **forecast**：预测、预报。IEA在2015年对2024年太阳能装机容量的预测，实际发展远超预期，说明可再生能源发展速度超出专家预期。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;"><span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">The oil-and-gas sector has underperformed</span> the broader <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">stockmarket</span> for the past 15 years, measured by <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">shareholder returns</span>. Unable to compete in the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">electrotech revolution</span>, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">it remains highly dependent on public subsidies</span> and <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">is now prioritising shareholder returns and debt reduction</span>—a sign of <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">structural retreat</span>. When the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">Paris agreement was gavelled</span>, heavy <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">upstream investment</span> in fossil fuels was the norm and <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">energy security</span> focused on <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">boosting supply</span>. Today, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">nearly 90% of upstream capital in the oil sector goes towards maintaining current production</span>, not increasing supply.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **stockmarket**：股市、股票市场。石油和天然气行业在过去15年的股价表现逊于整体股市，反映投资者对该行业长期前景的担忧。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **shareholder returns**：股东回报。包括股息和股价上涨，是衡量公司投资价值的重要指标。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **electrotech revolution**：电气技术革命。指以电力、电池、可再生能源为核心的能源技术革命，正在颠覆传统化石燃料行业。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **structural retreat**：结构性退却。指石油行业从扩张转向收缩的结构性变化，而非暂时的市场波动。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **gavelled**：敲槌、敲定。指会议主席用木槌敲击桌面，宣布会议结束或决议通过。此处指巴黎协定正式通过的时刻。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **upstream investment**：上游投资。指石油和天然气行业的勘探、开发和生产环节的投资，与下游（炼化、销售）相对。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **energy security**：能源安全。指国家确保能源供应稳定、可靠的能力，是国家安全的重要组成部分。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **boosting supply**：增加供应。传统能源安全策略强调增加产量，而新能源安全更注重多样化和本土化。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;"><span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">The question now is whether this exponential curve of clean-energy innovation can outpace the exponential curve of climate impacts</span>. Nowhere <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">is this more urgent than in Africa</span>—home to 60% of the world's "<span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">best solar resources</span>", according to the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">World Solar Council</span>, yet <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">recipient</span> of less than 3% of global <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">clean-energy investment</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">It is also subject to</span> some of the worst effects of climate change. By 2030 <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">investment in cleaner energy must triple</span> to around <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">$2trn</span> annually in <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">emerging and developing economies</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **best solar resources**：最佳太阳能资源。指太阳辐射强度高、日照时间长、适合大规模太阳能开发的地区。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **World Solar Council**：世界太阳能理事会，国际组织，致力于推动全球太阳能发展。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **recipient**：接受者、接收方。指非洲作为清洁能源投资的接受方，但获得的投资与其潜力极不匹配。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **clean-energy investment**：清洁能源投资。包括对太阳能、风能、储能等项目的资金投入。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **$2trn**：2万亿美元。trn是trillion的缩写，表示万亿。这是2030年新兴和发展中经济体每年需要的清洁能源投资规模。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **emerging and developing economies**：新兴和发展中经济体。指除发达国家外的所有经济体，包括中国、印度、巴西、非洲国家等。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;"><span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">There is not time to leave this to the market alone</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">Governments must align</span> public and private <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">finance</span> behind national <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">climate plans</span> that <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">prioritise</span> <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">bankable projects</span> with clear goals.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **finance**：资金、融资。包括公共资金（政府预算）和私人资金（企业投资、银行贷款等）。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **climate plans**：气候计划。各国根据巴黎协定提交的国家自主贡献（NDC），包括减排目标和具体措施。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **prioritise**：优先考虑、优先安排。强调政府需要将资金和政策重点放在最有价值的项目上。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **bankable projects**：可融资项目。指具有明确商业模式、能够吸引私人投资、风险可控的项目，与需要大量补贴的项目相对。</span>
</div>

<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0; font-size: 11.0pt;">So as <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">the world marks Paris+10</span>, <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">COP30 must be both a celebration and a reckoning</span>: a moment to <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">honour</span> progress, and to <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">recommit</span>, with clear eyes and steady <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">resolve</span>, to the <span style="color:#A24C4A; font-weight:bold; text-decoration:underline;">steeper climb</span> ahead. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">The era when American politics could make or break global climate co-operation is over</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">The world is no longer waiting for Washington</span>. <span style="font-weight:bold; font-style:italic;">This time the global south is leading the way</span>.</p>

<div class="note" style="background:#f5f5f5; border-left:4px solid #d9d9d9; padding:8px 12px; margin:12px 0; color:#666; font-size:10pt;">
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">备注：</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **Paris+10**：巴黎+10。指巴黎协定签署十周年，2025年是重要的里程碑。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **honour**：致敬、表彰。肯定过去十年的进步和成就。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **recommit**：重新承诺、再次承诺。强调在已有基础上进一步强化承诺和行动。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **resolve**：决心、决意。指面对更严峻挑战时的坚定意志。</span>
<span style="color:#808080; font-size:10.0pt;">- **steeper climb**：更陡峭的攀登。比喻未来减排任务的艰巨性，需要更大的努力和更快的速度。</span>
</div>
